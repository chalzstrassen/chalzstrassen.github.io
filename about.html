About Me

I am a Software Engineer currently based in Phoenix. I have lived in Baltimore for most of my life, but my desire to pursue web development has led me to where I am now. However, I do miss eating blue crabs native to the Baltimore region.

My entry into web development started when I became interested in Computational Biology. This led me to learn some Python on my own. By learning Python, I came across the Python web development framework Django and Flask. From there, I never looked back and jumped right into Ruby on Rails and Backbone.js.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching different kinds of sports including American Football, Basketball and Tennis. I don't usually follow any particular teams or players, but when I do , I follow the Baltimore Ravens.
